Are you a cutting edge technology company interested in aligning your brand with our thoughtfully curated Future of Computing conference? Why not consider sponsoring our event?

We are pleased to offer several sponsorship opportunities for companies who would like to support the emerging tech community.

There are Title and Virtual Conference sponsorship options for the greatest brand exposure during the event, as well as options for your logo to appear on creatively designed and sustainable conference merchandise. Finally, we also offer sponsorship options for Community, Investor, and Media sponsors to contribute to the conference in exchange for complimentary or discounted tickets and logo exposure on the website.

If you have any questions or are ready to secure your sponsorship slot, please contact us at sponsorships@causalislands.com.

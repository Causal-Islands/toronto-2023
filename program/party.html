Causal Islands Conference Party & Toronto Social

Join us for an evening social event for both Causal Islands conference attendees and the Toronto tech community.

‍

The party is Wednesday, April 26th, at the Lula Lounge (1585 Dundas Street West).

‍

Causal Islands covers the future of computing, and we’ll be showcasing some of the amazing conference speakers that blends art + tech. Toronto, meet the future of computing, Causal Islands, meet Toronto!

  • Doors at 6:30pm
  • 7pm Art + Tech Presentations featuring: Jenn Schiffer, Ramsey Nasser, David Ogborn, DJ @nineteenninetynix, & Special Guests

Jenn Schiffer

DIRECTOR OF COMMUNITY @ GLITCH + FASTLY

Ramsey Nasser

FOUNDING WORKER-OWNER @ EMMA TECHNOLOGY COOPERATIVE

David Ogborn

NETWORKED IMAGINATION LABORATORY @ MCMASTER UNIVERSITY
  • 8pm until late, music, mingle, food and drinks

Access to this event is included in your Causal Islands ticket. Those in Toronto who can’t make the full conference, tickets for just the Evening Social are available for $25, including drinks and food.

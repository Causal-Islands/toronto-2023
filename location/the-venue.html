The Paradise Theater

1006c Bloor St W
Toronto, ON M6H 1M2
View on Google Maps

A creative atmosphere that bristles at the status quo and longs for fresh and original ideas can often be a catalyst for innovation.

That is why we chose Toronto. The cinema, art, and music scenes in Toronto are bursting with kinetic energy. Cutting edge and experimental work easily reaches hungry and discerning audiences here. Choosing the Paradise Theatre was intentional - not just for its charm, beauty, and versatile spaces, but for the role it plays in the community as a creative hub.

The Paradise Theatre was built in the Art Deco and Art Moderne styles. Over its history it was an Italian filmhouse and a repertory and arthouse theatre. Today the Paradise is a multi-arts venue exhibiting films, concerts, and other live performances.

Getting Around the city

  • TTC - The Subway and Bus System
  • UP Express - Take this train from Pearson Airport to downtown Toronto or Union Station, a major transportation hub that links Toronto’s various neighbourhoods together.
  • Bike Share Toronto - Quick and hassle-free city-wide bike rentals
  • Uber and Lyft - Car rideshare options

‍

TIP: The UP Express train costs around $12.65 or less depending on the station. The ride from the airport to downtown is 20 minutes. Taxis from the airport are $65+ and driving takes longer than the train.
